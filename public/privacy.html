iLab Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

iLab ("the app") is a laboratory management platform. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account information: name and email address provided at sign-up.
  • Lab content: inspection records, equipment data, training certificates, project records, and files you upload.
  • Usage data: actions taken within the app (e.g. inspections completed, bookings made).

2. Google Drive Integration (optional)

If you choose to connect Google Drive as your personal file storage, iLab requests the drive.file scope. This scope allows iLab to:

  • Create and store files only in folders created by iLab (an "iLab Files" folder in your Drive).
  • Read and delete those same files when you request it.

iLab cannot access any other files in your Google Drive. We do not share your Drive files with third parties. Your Google OAuth token is stored only in your browser's local storage and never sent to iLab servers.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the lab management features of the app.
  • To allow your organization's administrators to review lab records.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

4. Data Storage

Lab content is stored in a Supabase-hosted database. Personal files may optionally be stored in your own Google Drive, OneDrive, or iCloud based on your storage preference. We do not transfer your data outside the service providers you choose.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting your organization administrator or using our contact form.

6. Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. Access to organizational data is restricted by role-based permissions.

7. Contact

For questions about this policy, please use our contact form. We aim to respond within 2 business days.

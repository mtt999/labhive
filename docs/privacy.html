Privacy Policy

LabHive · labhive.app · Last updated: June 2026

Introduction

LabHive ("LabHive", "the Platform", "we", "us", or "our") is an intelligent, all-in-one research lab management platform available at labhive.app. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, with whom we share it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using LabHive, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Platform.

Key point: We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising. Your lab data belongs to you and your organisation.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: Name, email address, and role, provided at sign-up or created by your lab administrator.
  • Profile data: Optional avatar, photo, and display preferences you set in your profile.
  • Lab activity data: Equipment bookings, inspection results, training certificates, project materials, maintenance records, inventory items, and barcode/QR records.
  • Equipment photos: Before/after condition photos uploaded as part of the booking process.
  • Files and documents: Training certificates, SOPs, project records, floor plans, and other documents you upload.
  • Messages: Messages sent between lab staff and users through the LabHive messaging feature.
  • Support requests: Subject, message, and contact email you provide when submitting a customer service request.
  • Technical data: Anonymous error reports and usage diagnostics used to improve platform stability.
  • Device and session data: Browser type, operating system, and session tokens stored locally to maintain your login session.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Within your organisation: Lab administrators and authorised members can access data within their own organisation's workspace.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database and file storage infrastructure), bound by data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of LabHive, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with prior notice.

4. Cloud Storage Integrations

  • Google Drive: LabHive stores files in a dedicated "LabHive Files" folder only. We do not access any other content. Revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
  • Microsoft OneDrive: Files stored in the app's designated AppFolder only. Revoke access from your Microsoft account settings.
  • WebDAV: Files stored on your configured server. LabHive does not store your WebDAV credentials on our servers.

Organisational files (SOPs, equipment photos, module images, floor plans) are always stored in LabHive's Supabase Storage.

5. Data Storage and Security

Platform data is stored in a Supabase-managed database hosted in the United States. We use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit, encrypted storage at rest, and role-based access controls. Despite these measures, no electronic storage method is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. Personal account data is deleted within 30 days of a verified deletion request. Backups may retain data for up to 90 days. To request deletion, go to the Profile tab in the app.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

LabHive uses browser local storage to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising cookies.

8. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data. Contact us via Customer Service and we will compile and send your data to your registered email address within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data via the Profile tab in the app. If you are a solo workspace owner with active teammates, your teammates will be notified and given the opportunity to take over ownership of the shared workspace data (excluding your personal profile information such as name, email, and password). If a teammate accepts the transfer, all shared data is preserved under their ownership. If all teammates decline or do not respond, all shared workspace data will be permanently deleted along with your account.
  • Restriction: Request limited processing in certain circumstances. Contact us via Customer Service and we will action your request within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format. Contact us via Customer Service and we will export and send your data as a structured file (JSON or CSV) to your registered email address within 3 to 5 business days.
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • CCPA opt-out: We do not sell personal information.

To exercise any right, contact us via Customer Service. We respond within 3 to 5 business days.

9. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights and freedoms, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours of becoming aware (as required under GDPR) and in compliance with applicable US state law.

10. Children's Privacy

LabHive is not directed to children under 13 (or 16 in the EU/UK). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages. If you become aware that a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored in the United States. If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, we ensure transfers comply with applicable data protection laws including Standard Contractual Clauses where required.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be noted by an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

13. Contact Us

For privacy questions or to exercise your rights, please use our Customer Service in the app.
